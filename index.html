Chocolate Bars

Chocolate is a delicacy that is enjoyed all over the world. Here, the focus is on cacao that has been made into dark chocolate. Cacao beans originate from many places around the globe, and there are many countries that manufacture dark chocolate. There are many elements that contribute to the quality of a chocolate bar including the ingredients, cocoa percentage, and flavours.

Cocoa Bean Origins

According to history, the cocoa tree is native to the Upper Amazon region of South America. With Venezuela, Ecuador, and Peru having some of the highest cocoa bean origins

for chocolate bars, we can see that the Americas produce some of the most cocoa beans.

Top 10 Chocolate Manufacturers

Interestingly, whilst the United States of America does not produce the most cocoa beans, it has a significantly greater number of chocolate manufacturers for dark chocolate than many other countries. Countries such as Ecuador and Peru have a lower number of manufacturers.

Cocoa Percentages

Chocolate bars can have different percentages of cocoa content. We can compare what percentages provide the best ratings in each year of review. Any rating about 3.5 means that chocolate bar is highly recommended and any bar with a rating over 4.0 is considered outstanding.
